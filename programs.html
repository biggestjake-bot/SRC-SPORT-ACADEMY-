Core Programs

Six established pathways for strength, artistry, and teamwork.

Existing academy programs offer a strong base for competitive athletes and beginners seeking confident entry into sport.

Football

Three weekly team sessions focused on conditioning, technical quality, and tactical understanding.

Rugby

Contact readiness, strength cycles, and team structure for resilient game-day performance.

Ballet

Grace, balance, posture, and performance confidence in a welcoming studio environment.

Table Tennis

Precision training for reaction time, spin control, and competitive match awareness.

Handball

Fast-tempo teamwork, throw mechanics, and coordinated attacking patterns.

Gymnastics

Foundational body control, mobility development, and progressive strength training.

Team Management

Each sport can maintain its own team list, training group, and competition readiness notes in a clear content structure.

  • Football: First team, development squad, goalkeeper unit.
  • Rugby: Contact prep, set-piece drills, leadership core.
  • Volleyball: Attack rotation, blockers, serve-receive group.

Weekly Schedules

Coaches can communicate session timing, court allocation, and special clinics using reusable schedule blocks.

  • Monday: Conditioning and tactical review.
  • Wednesday: Technical skill progression.
  • Saturday: Community sessions and match preparation.

Player Profiles

Profile cards can track attendance, position, strengths, development goals, and recovery focus in an easy-to-scan format.

Communication Flow

Trainers, students, and parents can quickly understand expectations, team roles, and upcoming coaching priorities.
