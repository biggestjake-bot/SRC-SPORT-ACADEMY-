About the Academy

Ambition with structure, discipline, and community impact.

SRC Sports Academy was created to strengthen Pentecost University’s sporting reputation while opening meaningful development pathways for students and the surrounding community.

Mission

To nurture confident, disciplined, and high-performing athletes through inclusive coaching, structured training, and a culture of academic and personal growth.

Vision

To become a leading regional sports institution known for talent development, vibrant community partnerships, and standout competitive results.

Values

Excellence, respect, resilience, teamwork, opportunity, and responsible leadership shape every athlete experience.

Funding & Support

Powered by strategic investment.

The academy grows through SRC leadership, community backing, and support from sports development bodies committed to youth talent, healthy lifestyles, and competitive excellence.

Growth Timeline

  • Launch: Academy introduced as a flagship SRC initiative.
  • Expansion: Wrestling, Taekwondo, Judo, and Volleyball added.
  • Future: Payment systems, athlete dashboards, and wider sponsorship support.
